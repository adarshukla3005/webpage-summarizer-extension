Universal Summarizer Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2023

This Privacy Policy explains how the Universal Summarizer extension ("we", "our", or "extension") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our browser extension.

Information We Collect

The Universal Summarizer extension may collect the following information:

  • Page Content: When you request a summary, we temporarily process the content of the current webpage to generate the summary.
  • URL Information: The URL of the page being summarized is processed to identify the content and provide relevant summaries.
  • User Preferences: Settings such as preferred summary length are stored locally in your browser.
  • User Feedback: If you provide feedback on summaries, this information is collected to improve our services.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To generate accurate summaries of web content
  • To improve the quality and accuracy of our summarization algorithm
  • To provide customized summary formats based on your preferences
  • To troubleshoot technical issues and improve the extension

Data Storage and Security

We prioritize your privacy and security:

  • Page content is processed temporarily and is not stored permanently on our servers once the summary is generated.
  • User preferences are stored locally in your browser using the extension's storage API.
  • We use industry-standard security measures to protect any data transmitted to our servers.
  • We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties.

Third-Party Services

Our extension uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Gemini API: Used for natural language processing to generate summaries. Please refer to Google's privacy policy for information on how they handle data.

Your Choices and Control

You have control over your data:

  • You can disable the extension at any time through your browser's extension management.
  • You can clear locally stored preferences through your browser settings.
  • You can choose not to provide feedback on summaries.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@universalsummarizer.com.
